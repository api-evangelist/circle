Circle argues that stablecoins function as supplementary payment infrastructure rather than replacements for traditional systems, citing 2025 data showing stablecoin transaction volume crossed the ACH network. Companies like Meta and DoorDash are integrating stablecoin settlements into existing workflows to improve capital efficiency in use cases where legacy rails are too expensive.
